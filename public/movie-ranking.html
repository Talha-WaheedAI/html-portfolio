The Best Movies According to Talha Waheed

My top 4 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

A moving tale of hope and perseverance breaking through despair.

Pulp Fiction

A genre-defining, nonlinear tapestry of sharp dialogue and iconic moments.

The Dark Knight

A dark, complex superhero saga elevated by an unforgettable villain.

The Godfather

An epic, immersive portrait of power, family ties, and moral ambiguity.
